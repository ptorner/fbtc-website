Venor was founded with the idea of providing companies, institutions and organizations with high quality services within the IT security area.

With extensive experience within the finance industry, governmental organizations, retail and other highly profiled companies Venor helps their customers to improve their security posture performing both proactive assessments, security architecture as well as incident response and forensics. Venor is an independent consultancy firm helping large organizations on a daily basis to protect their systems without compromising on the productivity.

Integrating security into the daily business and operations is a standard task. No matter if it includes creating incident handling procedures, security monitoring, interacting with regulatory bodies or assessing the current posture, Venor can help you and your organization to reduce the risk of a compromise, minimizing the damage of a breach or educating staff up to board level about IT security and the risks associated.
Venor also offers subscription services to contiously provide organisations with tailored security services to reduce risk and improve vigilance in the organization.
