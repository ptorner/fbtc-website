Venor offer select services to improve your organizations security posture, to reduce your organizations exposure and ensure incidents are handled in a correct and effecient manner.

Phishing awareness

Many attacks today are targeting users and phishing is one of the most common ways to target the users. With a phishing service from Venor your organization is tested to such threats on a regular basis. When a user clicks a link they will be sent to an educational page on how to detect phishing emails. The phishing tests varies in difficulty and after each test you will get a report with how many that clicked, inserted data or just opened the emails sent.

Venor Swift Response Services

Ensuring security incidents are handled properly is key to minimize the impact of a threat. With Venor Swift response services you will have our experts on site during an incidents, helping you perform forensics as well as leading the incident response effort.
With purposed designed gear our experts will arrive on site, collect data and analyze the situation and suggest a plan on how to handle the incident. We ensure the data is collected in a correct way, analyzed by an expert and reported to the appropriate stakeholders in your organization.


Our experts are only a phone call away, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.
